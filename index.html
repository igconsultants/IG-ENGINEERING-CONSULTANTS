About Us

IG ENGINEERING CONSULTANTS is a newly established civil engineering firm registered in Singapore. We provide consultancy services in civil, structural, and geotechnical engineering design for both temporary and permanent works, as well as conduct periodic structural inspections. Our team focuses on delivering innovative, safe, and practical solutions tailored to each project's needs. We handle BCA submissions and ensure full compliance with regulations, all while prioritizing client satisfaction, value-driven solutions, and timely project delivery. With a strong focus on quality and collaboration, we are dedicated to helping construction projects succeed safely, efficiently, and on schedule.
